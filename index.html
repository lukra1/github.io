Welcome

This mentorship guide provides a comprehensive, curated learning pathway designed to help you grow into a highly capable Data Analyst and confidently prepare for the PL‑300 Microsoft Power BI Data Analyst Associate exam. It brings together high‑quality, free training resources, hands‑on practice labs, and real‑world skill‑building activities that strengthen the core competencies every successful analyst needs—data preparation, modeling, visualization, analysis, storytelling, and Power BI solution design. Whether you're starting your analytics journey or advancing your professional expertise, this guide offers the structure, clarity, and practical experience required to build job‑ready skills and accelerate your career with confidence.

Created by Lucrecia Krause MVP — Senior Solutions Architect, Microsoft MVP, and leader of Microsoft User Group.
